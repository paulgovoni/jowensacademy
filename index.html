Dr. Jessie Owens Academy of Fine Arts

A Non-Profit Organization

1708 2nd St. NE * Winter Haven * FL * 33881

The purpose of Dr. Jessie Owens Academy of Fine Arts is to provide a community arts education program for low socio-economic and disadvantage children (elementary, middle, high school) and adults. Phase one will offer music lessons in choral and instrumental music. Students will be taught to read music, music theory and history, improvisation, present recitals, concerts and creative dance. Phase two will include drama.

Mission Statement

Our Mission is to improve lives through the arts by emphasizing that a well focused mind and positive disciplined behaviors develops a foundational pattern for excellence.
